Day Job

I am the owner of independent product design consultancy Scout Hawk Product Design Studio, where I have the good fortune to work with tech entrepreneurs who are trying to make the world a better place for everyone.

I also advise a number of companies like Broadly and SamaHope on product strategy and design.

Background

I've been a web design since I was young. I used to build HTML websites as a 12 year old with crude paint programs and hand-coded HTML. Fast-forward 18 years and I have the good fortune to still be doing what I love.

First and foremost, I am a designer. Though I have taken the time to hone my skills as a coder and a user researcher, I still take a creative, people-first approach to problem solving. I love blending disciplines to create experiences that make people smile.
