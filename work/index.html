What I Do

I'm a product designer, frontend developer, and entrepreneur focused on building apps and websites which improve quality of life for the greatest number of people.

I am the owner of independent product design consultancy Scout Hawk Product Design Studio, where I have the good fortune to build things for entrepreneurs who are trying to make the world a better place for everyone.

I also advise a number of companies like Broadly, Samahope, and Archively on product strategy and design.

Background

I've been a web designer since I was young. I used to build HTML websites as a 12 year old with crude paint programs and hand-coded HTML. Fast-forward 18 years and I have the good fortune to still be doing what I love — combining the technical with the creative.

Primarily, I think like a designer. While I spend an equal amount of time honing my design skills as I do my understanding of coding, user psychology, and business, I still take a creative, people-first approach to problem solving. I love to blend disciplines to create experiences that make people smile, and the capacity to create great experiences is why I love my job.
